Ultra-fast & Lightweight

Designed with performance in mind. Only the necessary data is loaded and rendered. Handles large datasets without slowing down your app.

Composable & Customizable

Easily adapt the calendar to your needs using flexible props like events, locale, components, onAddEvent, and more. Use out of the box or override parts as needed.

Seamless React Integration

Built 100% in React, this calendar fits naturally into your existing component tree. Fully typed, state-driven, and designed to work smoothly with modern React apps.

Styled with MUI, Ready to Theme

Uses Material UI components under the hood, making it easy to customize with your existing MUI theme. Responsive and visually consistent out of the box.
